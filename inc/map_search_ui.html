Help - Introduction

To change the origin of the search, click anywhere on the map. To change the radius and other search parameters, click on the 'Criteria' box.

Help - Search Results

You can now control the map to locate the properties that interest you.

Mousing over a pin on the map will show you brief details.

Clicking on a red pin on the map will add it to your basket, and turn the pin green. Clicking on a green pin will remove it from your basket.

To register for more information regarding properies in your basket, click on your basket and select 'Register'.
